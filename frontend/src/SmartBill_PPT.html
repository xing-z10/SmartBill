Challenges & Solutions

1  ·  Messy OCR output
Real receipts have inconsistent formats, mixed languages, and product codes. → Used Gemini's multimodal AI to extract structured fields even from noisy text.
2  ·  Informal voice instructions
"The drinks are on me" or "split the rest between us three." → Prompted Claude with full context: OCR items, group members, and "me/myself" → current user mapping.
3  ·  Identity alignment across accounts
"Me" in the creator's account must map correctly to the viewer's account. → Stored participant email alongside name; frontend matches by email to relabel each person's share.
